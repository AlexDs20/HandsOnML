\subsection{Perceptron}
Take as many weights as inputs, sum them and send them through step function $$\arrow$$ 0 or 1 (Heaviside step function).
\begin{equation}
    h_\mathbf{w}\(\mathbf{x}) = \mathrm{step}(\mathbf{x}^T\mathbf{w})
\end{equation}
